In The Beginning...

Oluwafemi Maxima was born into a family of five on August 6th 1880. He grew up in Modakeke, Osun state. As a child, maxima grew up as an easy-going but high-spirited child.

Life As Teenager:

In 1890,Maxima moved to Osogbo,where he enrolled in a private school as a primary (v) student. While he was a secondary school student,he enjoyed playing tasking games such as Monopoly and chess. Even though he had no special friend, he was an extrovert who tends to get into everybody's pool of attention within a short period of time. He graduated from the school in 1896 and proceeded into Osun state college of education for his NCE program which he completed in the year 1899. He later proceed to Ekiti State University where he studied Science Laboratory Technology.

Growing Up:

At age 20, Maxima moved to the city and he started a career out of what he has always loved to do - teaching. He learned to play some instrument within a short period of time, since he has always been a chorister from his childhood days,he found it easy to join any christian singing group and blend in wihin a short period of time.
